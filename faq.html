Frequently Asked Questions

What is Fuego Pilates?  

Fuego Pilates is a high-intensity Pilates studio that combines Pilates principles with vigorous exercises to provide a full-body workout aimed at improving strength, flexibility, and cardiovascular health.

What should I wear to a Fuego Pilates class? 

Wear comfortable, form-fitting workout clothes that allow for a full range of motion. Avoid loose clothing that might get in the way during exercises. Grip socks are required for better traction and safety.

Do I need to bring any equipment?

All necessary equipment, such as Pilates rings, balls, and Xformers, is provided by the studio. Please bring a water bottle and a small towel if you like.

Is Fuego Pilates suitable for beginners?

Yes, we offer classes for all fitness levels. Beginners are encouraged to arrive 10 minutes early to go over the Xformer. Beginners are encouraged to use the modifications suggested by the instructor throughout class.

How do I book a class?

You can book classes through our website. After your first time booking you will be directed to download our mobile app. We recommend booking in advance. Reservations open 1 week before start time.

What is your cancellation and no-show policy?

We have a strict 8-hour cancellation policy. If you need to cancel your reservation, please do so at least 8 hours before the class start time to avoid being charged a cancellation fee. If your reservation is canceled within 8 hours of class start time, you will lose the session if you are on a limited plan or class package, and charged a $15 late cancel fee if you are on an unlimited plan. If you are a no-show on a limited plan or class package you will forfeit the session. If you are a no-show on an unlimited plan you will be charged a $20 no show fee.

Are there any health restrictions for participating?

Participants should be in good health and free from any medical conditions that could be aggravated by physical activity. If you have any health concerns or conditions, please consult with your physician before starting classes.

Can I attend classes if I am pregnant?

Yes, but we recommend speaking with your physician and informing your instructor about your pregnancy so they can provide appropriate modifications to exercises.

Do you offer private sessions?

No, at this time we only offer group class format.
